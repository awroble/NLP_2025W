=================================================================================
DATA COLLECTION METHODOLOGY
=================================================================================

This dataset was created through comprehensive research of official WUT resources
and reliable information sources. The research process involved:

1. Systematic web searches for WUT information
2. Analysis of official WUT websites and documents
3. Review of student services, academic, and administrative information
4. Cross-referencing multiple sources for accuracy
5. Organization into thematic categories

   - Program types and offerings

2. Tuition & Financial (55 questions)
   - Tuition fees for EU and non-EU students
   - Scholarships and financial aid
   - Payment methods and costs
   - Student discounts

3. Academic Life & Studies (75 questions)
   - Academic year structure
   - Types of classes and examinations
   - Grading system and ECTS credits
   - Course registration and schedules

4. Language Requirements (45 questions)
   - English proficiency requirements
   - Language courses and exams
   - Foreign Language Centre services
   - Certificate exemptions

5. Student Services & Facilities (50 questions)
   - Campus locations and facilities
   - Library and IT services
   - Medical and psychological support
   - Student organizations

6. Accommodation & Housing (45 questions)
   - University dormitories
   - Application procedures
   - Contact information
   - Housing options in Warsaw

7. Visa & Immigration (45 questions)
   - Visa requirements and procedures
   - Residence permits
   - Meldunek registration
   - Legal stay requirements

8. Registration & Enrollment (40 questions)
   - Course registration procedures
   - USOS system usage
   - Add/drop periods
   - Token registration

9. Campus Life (35 questions)
   - Student activities and events
   - Sports and recreation
   - Warsaw city information
   - Cost of living

10. Administrative Procedures (26 questions)
    - Official communications
    - Document requests
    - Dean's Office services
    - Application submissions

11. General Information (4 questions)
    - University history and facts
    - Contact information
    - Location details

=================================================================================

- All information verified against multiple official sources
